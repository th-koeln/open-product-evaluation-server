Dashboard

The adminview represents the administration UI for creating and managing surveys. Here, users can see all of their surveys, domains and client. In addition they have following possibilities:

  • create a user
  • create a survey
  • create a domain
  • manage / update / delete users, surveys and domains
  • assign owned clients to owned domains
  • assign created surveys to owned domains
  • look up results (visualizations and raw data)
  • download results as .csv- or .pdf-file

The adminview is shipped with the backend. After starting this project, it is served at localhost:3000.
